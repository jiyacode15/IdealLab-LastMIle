Our Mission

We connect Paithani saree weavers and other rural artisans with markets across India and beyond, ensuring fair prices and timely delivery.

Digital Empowerment

Delivery Challenges

Poor Road Infrastructure

Many rural areas in Maharashtra lack proper roads, making vehicle access difficult especially during monsoon season.

Limited Digital Connectivity

Internet access is unreliable in remote villages, hindering online order management and tracking.

High Delivery Costs

Low order volumes and long distances make delivery economically challenging for small businesses.

Packaging & Handling

Delicate products like Paithani sarees require special packaging and careful handling during transit.

Survey Insights

Artisan Challenges

85% of Paithani weavers reported difficulty accessing markets outside their immediate region.

Delivery Time

Average delivery time to metropolitan areas is 7-10 days, compared to 2-3 days for urban businesses.

Digital Literacy

Only 35% of rural entrepreneurs feel confident using digital platforms for business operations.

Paithani Saree Heritage

The Art of Paithani Weaving

Paithani sarees are a traditional form of silk saree originating from Paithan in Maharashtra. Known for their intricate zari work and vibrant colors, each saree takes 2-6 months to complete depending on the complexity of design.

Artisan Stories

Meet the master weavers who have preserved this ancient craft through generations, now facing challenges in reaching global markets.

Delivery Support
